My Experience

Developing with Ruby and Rails since 2005 I have built a variety of live websites and web apps for Household Brands, Digital Agencies and Startups using Ruby and Rails. I use Agile processes, Behaviour/Test Driven Development, Git and Heroku or Brightbox to deliver stable, high-quality, maintainable code and big smiles!

I'm an active member of a number of local groups. I regularly attend North West Ruby User Group and co-started Manchester's BootStrap Business Club, a monthly gathering of entrepreneurs to discuss building sustainable businesses and projects.
